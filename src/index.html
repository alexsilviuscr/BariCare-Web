Types of interventions

Learn more about the top 3 most popular and effective interventions for weight loss.

01

Gastric sleeve

It is a weight loss procedure that involves reducing the size of the stomach by removing a large portion of it.

02

Gastric bypass

It is a weight loss procedure that involves creating a smaller stomach and rerouting the small intestine to this new stomach.

03

Gastric band

It is a weight loss procedure that involves placing an adjustable band around the upper part of the stomach to create a small stomach pouch.
